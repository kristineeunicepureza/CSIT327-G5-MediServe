Description
Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) is a widely used over-the-counter analgesic (pain reliever) and antipyretic (fever reducer). It's effective for treating mild to moderate pain and reducing fever. This medication is commonly used for headaches, muscle aches, arthritis, backaches, toothaches, colds, and fevers.
